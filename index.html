Project 2: Collective Digital Care

A drawing tool focused on care through language and communication.When we communicate online it's hard to understand body language, words can be misunderstood, and things left unsaid. Online communication is interpreted in the time and context of each individual, and thus it's harder to connect with other people. I wanted to focus on creating this individualized space for care, with this canvas as an expression of the mind: excessive, jumbled, vibrant, and loud. It includes excerpts from poems I wrote so that the user can now create a derivative poem of their own with a myriad of different words. Users can also icorporate their own words and phrases to create an art piece and type into the box to decorate the page. There are a multitude of combinations that can be achieved through exploration and the palette can be cleared with "c".
